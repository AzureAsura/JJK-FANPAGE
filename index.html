AWAKENING OF MALEVOLENCE

Curses lurk in the shadows, but one name reigns supreme—Sukuna, the King of Curses. As sorcerers and spirits clash, power alone won’t suffice. To survive, you must transcend limits or be consumed by the abyss.

Main Characters

Yuji Itadori

Itadori Yuji is a brave teen who becomes Sukuna’s vessel and fights curses to protect others in Jujutsu Kaisen.

Gojo Satoru

The strongest sorcerer with Six Eyes and Limitless. Cool, powerful, unstoppable.

Megumi Fushiguro

A calm and skilled sorcerer who uses shadow-based shikigami. Quiet, smart, and stronger than he lets on.

Nobara Kugisaki

A fierce sorcerer with nail-based attacks. Bold, stylish, and never afraid to fight on her own terms.

Honorable Mention

RYOMEN SUKUNA

Sukuna, known as the King of Curses, is a powerful and evil spirit in Jujutsu Kaisen. Once a deadly sorcerer, his 20 fingers became cursed objects after death. Now, he lives inside Yuji Itadori, causing chaos with his immense power, twisted personality, and thirst for battle.

Story

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes the host of a powerful Curse, Ryomen Sukuna, after swallowing a cursed object to save his friends. Instead of being executed, he's recruited by Satoru Gojo to join Tokyo Jujutsu High, where sorcerers train to fight deadly Cursed Spirits.

Alongside classmates Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, Yuji battles curses, uncovers dark secrets, and learns what it truly means to protect others. The story blends intense action, horror, and emotional moments in a world where life, death, and power constantly collide.
